Kratom's primary alkaloid, mitragynine, acts on adrenergic receptors at low doses. Research published in the *Journal of Psychoactive Drugs* (2018) confirmed that at doses below 5 grams, mitragynine produces stimulant-like effects — increased alertness, elevated mood, reduced fatigue, and enhanced motivation.
